Hotel Alexander features an Italian restaurant, stylish cocktail bar and modern rooms. In the lively Victoria district, the hotel is a 2-minute walk from London Victoria Station and a 5-minute walk from Buckingham Palace. The spacious, air-conditioned rooms at the modern Park Plaza Victoria London feature a flat-screen TV with on-demand movies. All rooms have a work desk and free WiFi. A sauna and steam room are available for guests to relax in the Park Plaza Leisure Club. A fully equipped gym is also available. TOZI is a lively Italian restaurant and bar, which specialises in small Venetian dishes called cicchetti and also serves classic Italian cocktails. The lounge bar serves drinks, snacks and coffee throughout the day and evening. The hotel is centrally located, around 1 mile from Parliament Square, Westminster Abbey and Big Ben.
